Brief Bio

Currently I am a Ph.D graduate student research assistant at Case Western Reserve University where I work under M. Cenk Cavusoglu in the Medical Robotics and Computer Integrated Surgery (MeRCIS) laboratory. I am the first author on two papers which were presented at ICRA 2012 in Minneapolis, Minnesota and ICRA 2013 in Karlsruhe, Germany. My duties in the lab include personnel management, resource managment, marketing, and recruitment. I graduated from Rose Hulman Institute of Technology in May of 2009 with a B.S. in electrical engineering and a B.S. in Mathematics.
